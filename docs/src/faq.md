# FAQ and gotchas

**I checked a habit off. Why isn't it "completed" in Life?**
Round checkbox = "done *today*", and today *is* logged (streak, heatmap). A habit never completes by checkbox — it repeats; that's its job. To end it for good, open it and *Retire habit*. One-time things (square checkbox) do complete everywhere at once. See [The completion rule](./completion.md).

**I set something to "Today" — does it also need a repeat?**
No. *Today* alone puts it on the list until you complete it. A repeat alone puts it there on schedule. They're mutually exclusive on purpose — each gives the checkbox a different meaning, so choosing one clears the other.

**Something I expected isn't in this week's list.**
Week/month/quarter/year lists are real calendar instances. A goal tagged "this week" *last* week sits in last week's list — step the arrows back and you'll find it, then *→ Today* or re-schedule it into the current week. Old items from before this existed appear in the current period.

**Yesterday's unfinished task is showing today. Bug?**
Feature: tasks carry forward until done ("carried from Mon"), only on the real today. Finish it today and both days stay honest: planned Monday, done Wednesday.

**Where did my completed things go?**
Nowhere — completed items stay in their lists, struck through. Lists with a lot done offer *Hide completed* under Sort.

**I moved something and the area changed by itself.**
Picking a parent adopts the parent's life area — living inside something means living where it lives. Override with the area chips in the same Move sheet before pressing Move; nothing applies until then.

**What's the difference between a note and a "someday" goal?**
A note is thinking (no checkbox, no guilt). Someday is a shelved *intention* — still a goal, just not now. If it will eventually be *done*, make it a goal; if it's words you want to keep, it's a note.

**My phone edit isn't on my laptop.**
Both signed in? Then tap the 🔄 sync button (or just refocus the tab — it pulls automatically). Not signed in = each device is its own island by design.

**Markdown symbols are showing as plain text.**
You're looking at the **Write** pane — tap **Preview** (or widen the window; desktop shows both). The symbols are supposed to stay visible where you type.

**Can I turn a note into a goal? A goal into a habit?**
Yes and yes. *Organize into something* on a note; *Edit* on anything else. Nothing about a thing is permanent — kind, tracker, area, parent, horizon all change anytime.

**What do the free limits actually stop?**
Creating a 5th area or a 41st active item. Completed and someday items don't count against you. Everything you already made keeps working.

**I deleted something by accident.**
Settings → Trash → restore, within 7 days (30 on Premium).
