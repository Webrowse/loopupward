# Life: everything you're growing

Life is the map. Where Plan asks *"what today?"*, Life asks *"what, at all?"* — every goal, habit, and project you're carrying, arranged by the part of life it serves.

## Areas

Areas are the top level: Health, Career, Family, Money, French, whatever your life is actually made of. Each area page lists everything filed under it with a progress bar per item. Items don't need an area ("not filed anywhere yet" collects the loose ones), but areas are what make five hundred ambitions feel like six shelves.

## Things (items)

Everything you create is the same kind of object wearing different clothes. When you add something you pick what it is:

- **Goal** — has an end. "Run a half marathon." Done when it's done.
- **Habit** — has no end. "Meditate." Lives on repeats; measured in streaks.
- **Project** — a container of steps. "Renovate kitchen."
- **Book** — tracked chapter by chapter.
- **Milestone** — a marker inside something bigger. "First 10k run."
- **Note / Folder** — live in Notes (see that chapter).
- Quotes, principles, lessons, memories, ideas, dreams — keepsakes; they live in a quiet space of their own, out of the execution flow.

Don't overthink the choice — you can change kind, area, parent, and everything else later. Nothing is locked.

## How a thing is measured

Each thing tracks progress in one of a few ways:

| Tracker | Feels like | Example |
|---|---|---|
| **Done / not done** | A checkbox | "Get the visa" |
| **Counter** | n of a target | 132 / 200 workouts |
| **Book** | chapter n of m | chapter 7 / 20 |
| **Money** | amount saved | ₹25,000 / ₹100,000 |
| **Percent** | your own estimate | 45% of the course |
| **Habit** | days logged, streaks | meditated 12 days straight |
| **Nothing** | it just exists | a note |

## Nesting: things inside things

Any thing can live inside any other. This is the app's spine:

```
🎯 Reach conversational Spanish        (year)
   🎯 Finish beginner course           (this quarter)
      📖 Textbook, 20 chapters         (book: chapter 9/20)
   🔁 15 minutes of practice           (habit, daily)
```

**Progress flows upward automatically.** A thing with its own tracker reports that; a thing without one averages its children. Tick the textbook forward and the course, and the year goal above it, both move. You never do bookkeeping upward.

Open any item to see what's inside it, its history, streaks and heatmap (for habits), and the *"break off a piece for today"* box — the bridge to Plan, covered in the next chapter.

## Moving things

*Move* on an item's page relocates it: pick a **life area** and/or the thing it should **live inside**. The list shows everything you have (goals and projects first, searchable) with each candidate's area and time horizon, so you can tell your yearly goals apart. Picking a parent also adopts that parent's area, which is almost always what you meant — override it with the chips if not. **Nothing moves until you press Move**; Cancel really cancels.

## Completed, someday, trash

- **Mark complete** retires a thing with dignity — struck through, still visible, reopenable.
- **Someday** shelves a thing you're not working on without deleting the ambition.
- **Delete** moves it to Trash (in Settings), recoverable for 7 days (30 with Premium). Children of a deleted thing move up a level — they aren't lost.
