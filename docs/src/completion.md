# The completion rule

One rule, learned once, true everywhere:

> **Checking a thing checks the thing itself — everywhere it appears.**
> A **square** checkbox completes the thing for good. A **round** checkbox logs *this day* of a repeating thing.

## Square: one-time things

Tasks, goals, milestones. Check one — on Today, in a Life area, inside a parent goal, in a week list — and it is done in all of those places at once, struck through but still visible. Check again to undo. The same tap as the *Mark complete* button on the item's own page.

**And it flows down and up:**

- Completing a task that was the **last open piece of a goal** completes the goal too. You did the thing; the app does the filing.
- Completing a child updates the parent's percentage — a parent measured by its children moves the moment any of them does.

## Round: repeating things

Habits and anything with a schedule. The circle means "done *today*": it feeds the streak and the heatmap, and tomorrow the circle returns empty, because the habit isn't finished — that's the point of a habit. Some repeats count more than once a day ("3 glasses of water") — the circle fills as a ring, 2 of 3.

Ending a habit forever is a deliberate act (*Retire habit* on its page), never a side effect of doing it today.

## Why both exist

If one checkbox meant both "done forever" and "done today," you couldn't trust it — doing your pushups once would delete the pushups habit. The shape tells you what your tap will mean *before* you tap.

This is also why a thing can't simultaneously repeat *and* be marked "do today": the two give the checkbox different meanings, so setting one clears the other (a repeat reaches Today by itself anyway).

## Quick reference

The two symbols below are the two checkbox shapes you'll see in the app itself:
**☐ square** (one-time — completing) and **◯ round** (repeating — logging today).

| You check… | What happens |
|---|---|
| ☐ a one-time task on Today | Task done. If it was its goal's last open piece, the goal completes too |
| ☐ a goal in a Life list | Goal completes, everywhere. Tap again to reopen |
| ◯ a habit, anywhere | Today logged; streak +1; back tomorrow |
| ☐ a task with "+2 chapters" | Task done and the book meter moves by 2 |
| ◯ a habit you meant to end | Just logs today — use *Retire habit* on its page to end it |
